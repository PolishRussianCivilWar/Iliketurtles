Why Hawksbill Turtles Matter

Hawksbill turtles are crucial for the health of coral reefs. By feeding on sponges, they prevent the reefs from being overgrown, allowing marine life to thrive :0

Threats to Hawksbill Turtles

Climate Change

Rising sea levels and temperatures affect nesting beaches and the gender balance of hatchlings

Illegal Trade

Hawksbill turtles are hunted for their beautiful shells, used in jewelry and ornaments :(

Pollution

Marine debris, especially plastics, poses a significant threat to their survival. :(

How You Can Help

  • Participate in beach clean-ups.
  • Spread awareness about the dangers of plastic pollution.
  • Support conservation organizations dedicated to protecting marine life.
